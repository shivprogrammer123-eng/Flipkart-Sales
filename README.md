Excel Sales Dashboard README
Project Overview

A compact Excel sales dashboard built with pivot tables and charts to analyze revenue across occasions, cities, categories, products, months, and hours of the day. Designed for quick business review and decision making.
Key Features

    13 pivot tables powering interactive analysis.

    Revenue breakdowns by Occasion (Birthday, Holi, Valentine’s Day, etc.), City (Agra, Dhanbad, Bilapur, etc.), Category, Month, and Hour of day.

    Top 5 products by revenue and Top cities by orders.

    KPI cards and slicers for dynamic filtering by date, occasion, and other dimensions.

    Time‑of‑day revenue trend and monthly revenue trend charts.

Data and Methodology

    Source data: transactional sales table with order timestamp, city, product, category, occasion, and revenue.

    Calculations implemented via pivot tables and calculated fields to derive revenue by dimension and time bucket.

    Slicers linked to pivot tables for synchronized filtering and fast exploration.

How to Use

    Open the workbook in Excel.

    Enable content and refresh all pivot tables.

    Use slicers to filter by date range, occasion, city, or category.

    Click chart elements or pivot rows to drill into top products or hourly trends.

Business Insights Delivered

    Revenue drivers by occasion and city to inform promotional planning.

    Peak selling hours and months to optimize staffing and campaign timing.

    Product ranking to prioritize inventory and merchandising.
